Students & First Time Voters

Voting is EASY. Here's a step by step guide!

Step 1: Register Online

Register to vote using this online web form.

If you live in two places (school vs parents' house) you can choose either.

Step 2: Look Up Where to Vote

Enter your postal code here then click 'Where do I vote?', or call 1-800-463-6868 and they will sort you out.

Notice that there's no complicated documents involved or mailing of anything anywhere. You can do this in 5 minutes using your phone.

Step 3: Gather Your ID

Here's the ID you need to vote. You do have it, even if you're a student living in rez.

The number one problem at this step is that your "official" ID has your parents' address, but you live away at school. Don't worry, you're still good to go. Just bring a second piece of ID that has your name and current address on it. It can be as simple as any document or letter issued by your university or rez. Worst case, ask your don for a letter confirming your address, which they can provide you in no time.

Step 4: Show Up and Vote on Oct 19, 2015

It's quick, easy, and not scary. Someone will meet you at the door and show you just where to go and what to do. It's usually a sweet old lady.

Last time I voted, one of the poll workers gave me a home baked chocolate chip cookie, straight up. It's that chill.

Compact Checklist
  • Register online to vote
  • Look up where to vote
  • Gather your ID
  • Show up and vote on Oct 19, 2015

No complicated documents needed. No mailing forms anywhere. No excuses.
